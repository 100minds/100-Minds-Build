100 Minds & Temple Street

For our first year running 100minds is partnering with Temple Street Children’s Hospital. Temple Street hospital cares for and treats over 130,000 sick Irish children every year - and it needs our help.

As the first port of call for tens of thousands of children every year suffering from every kind of illness and injury imaginable we believe that we can achieve great impact with Temple Street to significantly improve the hospitals infrastructure, enhance patient quality of life and support families in need.

In the early stages of the 100minds journey Temple Street assigned Laila McDonald, a dedicated fundraising executive for the hospital to our cause. With Laila’s help we were able to work out a full spend agreement that would aim to holistically improve the hospital not just with bulk machine purchases but also by focusing on other key areas such as improving hospital environment and backing new programmes such as the hardship fund that covers cost support for families finding it difficult to keep up with hospital payments. As transparency is part of our mission at 100minds we have included this full spend breakdown below. There are three categories, green covers key machine purchases, the blue category is focused on patient well being and finally the orange category is geared towards supporting families in need.

We are delighted to be partnering with 100minds to work towards this incredible objective. It’s great to see students channeling their energies and their initiative into something special in order to help sick and seriously ill children. I would encourage as many students as possible to get involved and help buy vital life saving equipment and show their support for our little patients.

Denise Fitzgerald Temple Street Children's Hospital
