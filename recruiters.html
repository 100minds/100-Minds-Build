What Do Recruiters Think?

LinkedIn is about Transformation – transformation of self, transformation of the company, and transformation of the world. Our mission is to hire individuals who are passionate about coming on this journey to change the world. 100minds is a fantastic opportunity to build your professional brand, collaborate with like-minded people, and ultimately to highlight your leadership, entrepreneurial and creative skills toemployers in companies like LinkedIn. 100minds is a fantastic opportunity to build your professional brand, collaborate with like-minded people, and ultimately to highlight your leadership, entrepreneurial and creative skills to employers. 100minds allows students to achieve all of this while consequently delivering an amazing result for the chosen charity.

Lindsay Browning Talent Acquisition Manager, LinkedIn

At KPMG, we are always looking to recruit bright and ambitious graduates. Students that partake in the 100minds initiative are showing that they can bring something additional to us. To achieve the 100minds challenge, you will need strong communication skills, planning & organisation skills and commitment to task – all skills that KPMG value when we look for potential employees. I think it is a great initiative to get involved in and this entrepreneurial spirit and willingness to give something back is aligned to the KPMG values.

Paul Vance HR Director, KPMG

Getting a new grad position at Google is not all about the grades. While grades are important, we take a far more holistic view when reviewing candidates. We’re looking for people who have a variety of strengths and passions, not just isolated skill sets. Graduates that have a passion for the online industry and someone that has made the most of their time at University are interesting to us. This can be demonstrated through their involvement in clubs/societies and any leadership positions they may have assumed. We want to get a feel for what makes a candidate unique.

Deborah O'Neill University Programmes Specialist, Google

At Deloitte, we look for well-rounded individuals who are passionate about making an impact every day through meaningful value added work. We look for a range of transferable skills when recruiting graduates, including problem solving skills, communication skills, team working ability, commercial awareness and more. We believe getting involved in 100minds will help you develop some of the key skills necessary to develop a successful career in any organisation and is a great opportunity to gain a broader view of the world. 100minds can help you to differentiate yourself amongst your peers when looking for a job as a graduate, by demonstrating your willingness to go above and beyond, and commitment to getting involved in something you feel is important.

Rosemary Hogan Deloitte HR

At Microsoft Ireland we are passionate about making a difference in our community, so we fully support the 100minds Initiative. It’s a great example of people combining their talent with the desire to make a difference and they are the kind of people we want to join us.

Joe ffrench HR Director, Microsoft Ireland

PwC is Ireland's leading professional services firm and each year we recruit approximately 250 graduates. Throughout our recruitment process we look for students who have developed strong skills in areas such as communication, team work and leadership, needed for success in the workplace. These skills are often developed through active involvement in clubs, societies, sports and through volunteering with initiatives such as 100minds. My advice to prospective students is to get involved at University – the key to success is getting the right blend of not only academic success, but also well-developed interpersonal skills.

Susan Kilty HR Partner, PwC

The 100minds initiative is an opportunity for ambitious, altruistic under-graduates to showcase their creativity and determination to help improve the lives of children. For Wayra, 100minds participants are demonstrating their entrepreneurial abilities as well as the skills required to conceive and deliver upon a strategy, all important elements in the leaders we bring into Wayra. We've already invested in startups which emerged straight from university so 100minds membership will become an achievement we'll be watching out for

Karl Aherne Wayra Ireland
