100minds Timeline

October 1st: Applications Open (Successful applicants notified on a rolling basis). October 31st: Applications Close. November 11th: Fundraising begins! Participants receive their 100minds profile page to accept online donations. February 17th: Fundraising ends! Cash donated to Temple Street. February 21st: Awards ceremony and closing party hosted by Signature Group.

The 100minds Challenge

Each person that takes part in 100minds is set the target of fundraising €1,000. The goal of the project is to collectively fundraise €100,000 and to donate this sum of money to one charity or cause.

The €1,000 Goal

The individual €1,000 goal is simply a target to encourage participants to think big! As a group we’re very focused on individuals gaining new skills from taking part. We’d be just as happy if someone raised a €100 and gained experience in event management as a result!

100minds Mentorship

Undergraduates are encouraged to think creatively when brainstorming for potential fundraising ideas. They are supported in doing this through the 100minds mentorship program. Mentors are recent graduates now working in PwC, Google, LinkedIn, Microsoft, KPMG and Deloitte. Each mentor will be assigned a group of 10 participants. The role of the mentor will be to support participants through the phases of project management with their fundraising.

Your 100minds Profile Page

Individual fundraising begins on the 11th of November. Each participant is then provided with a 100minds.org profile page. You can use your 100minds profile to keep track of your fundraising project, post updates, accept donations from friends online and blog about some of the challenges you faced.
